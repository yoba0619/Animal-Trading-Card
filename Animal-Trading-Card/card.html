Bottlenose dolphin

Known for their playful behavior, dolphins are
highly intelligent. They are as smart as apes, and the evolution of their larger brains is surprisingly similar to humans.

  • Scientific Name: Tursiops
  • Average Length: 13 feet
  • Average Lifespan: 40 years
  • Habitat: Warm and temperate seas worldwide

Their color is dark gray on the back and lighter on the flanks. They weigh an average of 660 pounds and reach pver 13 feet length. They live in warm and temperate seas worldwide except Arctic and Antarctic Circle regions.
